Loving/Leaving

It’s easier to miss people than loving them
I give away goodbyes like candy on halloween
Leaving before I’m left
Hold my heart tight, lest it falls behind
Cinderella’s shoe at the ball
Too easy, a story, too happy
Looking for the perfect fit
In a kingdom of strangers
To be alone with, together
For tonight, a lifetime
It’s easy to forgive almost everyone, but myself
So I’ve learnt to say sorry in seven languages
But love, in only yours
